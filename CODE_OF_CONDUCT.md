# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

Contributors, users, and maintainers are expected to:

- stay respectful and direct
- focus disagreements on technical content and behavior
- keep bug reports and reviews concrete, reproducible, and scoped
- respect privacy and avoid sharing someone else's private data without consent

## Unacceptable behavior

This project does not accept:

- harassment, threats, or intimidation
- hate speech or discriminatory language
- sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention
- doxxing or publication of private information
- spam, trolling, or deliberate disruption of technical discussion

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, end discussions, reject contributions, or block
participants who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you need to report conduct problems, use a private maintainer contact path.
If you do not already have one, request a private channel without posting the
details publicly.
